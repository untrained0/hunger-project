Our Mission

HELP DONATE TO THOSE WHOSE IN HUNGER!

Share A Meal is a holistic initiative to provide free meals for poor people across India. The program supports serving people thereby offering different menus each day. Donate a meal to the Share A Meal Program,transforming Hunger into hope.


FEED MORE. WASTE LESS.

We are on a mission to end waste,fight hunger and feed communities using the power of Technology and Logistics. Through our national network ,we're also helping partners accross country to dramatically reduce their waste. Our works are designed to provide essential food support to underserved areas in the form of food or packaged food products depending on the need. Our goal is to ensure, Feed people not landfills.


Reduce Your Waste for Good

We work with businesses, foundations, governments, and NGO teams to provide food to local needy communities. Get involved through our Pop-Up Meal Delivery services.
